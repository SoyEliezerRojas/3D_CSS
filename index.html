Digital Value to Society

Value Impact for
Society & Environment

Lower Carbon Emissions:

Lower consumption of agricultural chemicals (fertilizers and pesticides) and water through precision application, will lead to lower environmental imbalance and reduced carbon emission. As farmland becomes more efficient, the need for new farmland (and deforestation) will also reduce.

Live saved

Higher farm productivity will also lead to lower hunger and malnutrition.

Value Impact
For Consumer

Coast Saving:

Higher agricultural yield will increase crop production and can potentially reduced food prices for end customers

Value Impact
For Labor

Income growth:

Farmers can increase their income as agricultural yield goes up and overall costs go down.

Net Job Creation:

This will also create new job opportunities in technology sectors for development of sensors, analytics and software. Further, additional resources will be required to spread awareness about the availability and use of new technology.
